ABOUT US

Inspired by the tale of Chinnasami Selvam and
being biryani lovers, we sought to rediscover
the legendary taste and bring it to you.

Legend has it that Chinnasami Selvam
was known as Thalaivar by his community
for his truly OUTSTANDING biryanis.
Born of a Tamil lineage that had migrated
to Nellore for opportunities, the ambitious
young Chinnasami moved from Nellore to
work in the kitchens of the Nizam ruler of
Hyderabad nearly 100 years ago.
His talents as a cook quickly brought
him acclaim and moved him through the
ranks as his culinary innovations delighted
the palettes of the court.

After 17 glorious years, he returned to Nellore
to start his family. With him, he brought
the recipes he had created for the Nizams
and perfected his 21 spice
masala by incorporating the traditions
of Nellore and his Tamil roots.

Strong roots, flavourful biryanis.
